What's inside

One system for the whole program.

Whelpora doesn't do your tax return or reply to your buyers. It holds the structure — the records, the milestones, the paperwork — so you stop holding it in your head.

01
Every puppy, every milestone, one place.

From planned breeding to last placement. Weights, care plans, photos and reminders — all tied to the litter, all ready when the vet or buyer asks.

02
Health records that find themselves.

Vaccinations, vet visits, medications and genetic testing, attached to each dog and puppy. No more scrambling through emails at 9 pm to find a vax date.

03
Paperwork that stays on top of itself.

Invoices, expenses and contracts — tracked in one place, ready for your accountant. Build your own contract templates once, reuse them forever.

04
Buyers and deposits — no crossed wires.

Know exactly where every buyer is. Interested, deposit paid, matched to a puppy, pickup, gone home. One source of truth per litter.

05
A calendar that knows what's due.

Heats, matings, vet visits, whelping milestones, deworming weeks. The system pulls from every dog and litter and tells you what today actually looks like.

06
The program, not just the paperwork.

Pedigree, genetics, coat predictor, guardian homes, retained puppies. The tools serious breeders actually use, built in — not bolted on.
