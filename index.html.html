What's inside

Everything your kennel needs. Nothing it doesn't.

01
Litter management

From planned breeding to last placement. Auto-generated timelines, puppy weights, care plans and milestone reminders — all in one place.

02
Health records

Vaccinations, vet visits, medications and genetic testing — attached to each dog and puppy. Never scramble for a health record again.

03
Paperwork & finances

Invoices, contracts, expenses and receipts — generated, sent and tracked in one place. See your profit position at a glance, any time.

04
Customer management

Buyer enquiries, waiting lists, deposit records and communications — organised and clear. Know exactly where every buyer is in the process.

05
Tasks & calendar

A daily checklist that knows what's due for each dog and litter. Reminders for heats, vet appointments and whelping milestones.

06
Dog profiles

Every dog in your program — full history, health records, breeding data, photos and documents — stored cleanly and accessible anywhere.

Finances

Run your breeding program like a business.

Most breeders manage a serious operation from a spreadsheet and a folder of receipts. Whelpora gives you a real financial picture — receivables, expenses, profit — without the complexity.

  • Generate professional invoices directly from litter records
  • Track expenses by category with receipt attachments
  • See income, expenses and net position at a glance
  • Digital contracts with e-signature — no printing required
